Why We Built SkillSync

In 2023, we spoke with over 100 HR leaders at companies ranging from 50 to 5,000 employees. The problem was universal: Individual Development Plans were either non-existent or a time-consuming burden.

Managers would spend 2-3 hours per employee crafting IDPs, often relying on generic templates that didn’t align with company resources or the proven 70-20-10 learning model. By the time Q2 rolled around, most IDPs were abandoned or forgotten.

Meanwhile, employees craved growth opportunities. LinkedIn’s Workplace Learning Report found that 94% of employees would stay longer at a company that invested in their development. The disconnect was clear: companies wanted to develop talent, but lacked the time and tools to do it effectively.

That’s when we realized AI could solve this. Not generic ChatGPT prompts, but purpose-built AI trained on your company’s specific context using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). An AI that could learn your competency frameworks, integrate with your course catalogs, and generate truly personalized development plans in minutes.

SkillSync was born from this insight. We partnered with learning science experts to encode the 70-20-10 model into our algorithms. We built privacy-first architecture to ensure GDPR compliance. And we designed an interface so intuitive that HR teams could deploy it company-wide in days, not months.
