Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later? Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial for the Growth plan? Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all Growth plan features. No credit card required.

What counts as an “employee” for billing? An employee is any individual with an active account who has generated or accessed an IDP in the current billing period.

Do you offer non-profit or education discounts? Yes! We offer 30% discounts for qualified non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Contact our sales team for details.
