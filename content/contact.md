## Frequently Asked Questions

**How quickly can we get started?**
Most teams are up and running within 1-2 days. Our onboarding process is designed to be simple and fast.

**Do you offer custom integrations?**
Yes! Enterprise customers can integrate SkillSync with systems like Workday, SAP SuccessFactors, and custom HRIS platforms.

**Is there a minimum contract length?**
Our Starter and Growth plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Enterprise plans typically involve annual contracts.

**Can we try before we buy?**
Absolutely! We offer a 14-day free trial of our Growth plan with full access to all features. No credit card required.
